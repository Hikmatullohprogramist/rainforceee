BALCONY RESTORATION

Severe weather. Old age. Poor installation. Inappropriate design. Whatever the cause, concrete and railing deterioration can quickly affect the structural integrity of the balconies, which not only raises safety concerns but also diminishes property value. From concrete deterioration, corrosion of railing supports, water penetration to spalling and cracks, we know the issues, and we know the solutions. With an understanding of the budgetary and logistical demands of busy settings and occupied spaces, we have helped many building owners and managers restore the structural integrity of their balconies by offering consistent good work for a fair price.
The rainForces balcony restoration solutions include:
 

♦   Balcony Slabs and Shearwalls Restoration

♦   Railing Systems Replacement

♦   Pedestrian Traffic Coatings and Membrance
