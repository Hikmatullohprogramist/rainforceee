CAULKING & SEALANT

Achieving the full expected service life of a sealant requires a combination of correct joint design, appropriate sealant selection, product performance testing, appropriate surface preparation, and conformance to manufacturers’ guidelines and industry standards.

Our caulking and sealant services include:

  • Identification of the cause of sealant failure
  • Restoration any of the original design and/or installation flaws
  • Selection of an appropriate sealant that best accommodates a particular installation
  • Testing of sealant on-site
  • Substrate preparation in accordance with the sealant manufacturer’s specifications
  • Installation of backer rods and application of selected sealant
  • Troubleshooting during application of sealant to avoid failures
